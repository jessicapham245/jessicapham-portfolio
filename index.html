Hello! Welcome to my portfolio!

I am 20 years old and becoming a tattoo artist has been my dream career since I was 14. I currently attend college at University of California of Riverside and for a long time I had trouble deciding my major because tattoing was my "Plan A" since the beginning. I've currently decided to pursue a political science - administrative studies degree so that one day I'd be successful in opening my own inclusive and socialist shop. Through my passion, I have some adequate knowledge on the tattoo industry by doing my own research. I read up on everything from Tebori to traditional Moko, and I recognize the revered history and techniques of tattooing. In my practice for the future, I've use temporary "tattoo" henna (Inkbox, not sponsored) on my friends and family, and below is my online portfolio where I show case a few pieces I've done.
